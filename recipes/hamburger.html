Hamburger

Here is by far the best homemade hamburger recipe you'll find on the internet. It takes what makes a hamburger good and add some others things to spice it up.

The visual and the aspect of a classical burger but with a taste that is more special !

ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • 250 g beef patty
  • onions
  • 2 slices of raclette cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. First, put some butter in a pan and add the onions to make them caramelized
  2. Once they're done, put them on a plate and make two meatballs with the patty adding some salt and pepper
  3. Put them on the plate and make it grill on the two sides
  4. Once they're brown, reduce the heat , put the once slice of cheese on each patty and put on the pan lid
  5. Wait until the cheese is melted
  6. Put the burger buns in the toaster
  7. Finally, put all the ingredients between the buns and Enjoy !!
