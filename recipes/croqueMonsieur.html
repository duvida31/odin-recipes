ingredients

  • 4 slices of breads
  • 2 slices of the cheese of your choice (we use some Maredsoushere)
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 100 g of mozzarella
  • butter

Steps

  1. First, put some butter on the bread
  2. cut the mozzarella into thin slices
  3. cover two slices of bread with ham, then, one slice of cheese, then again a slice of ham and finally the mozzarella
  4. cover it with the two other slices of bread
  5. put them in a Croque-Monsieur toaster or toast it with a pan and butter. Enjoy !!
