ingredients

  • 250 g of chicken breast
  • 100 g of mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • 200 g of tomato sauce
  • Spices
  • Flour
  • breading
  • Two eggs

Steps

  1. Make the tomato sauce boil and add salt pepper, basilic, oregano and a cube of sugar
  2. Cut the chicken into thin slices
  3. Scramble the eggs
  4. Dip the chicken slies in the flour then the eggs and in the breading
  5. Cook the chicken slices
  6. Put the sauce on top of the chicken slices
  7. Cut the mozzarella and put it on top
  8. Cover it with a lot of parmesan
  9. Put it in the oven at 200 Â° until the cheese has melted and Enjoy !!!
